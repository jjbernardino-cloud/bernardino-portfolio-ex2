About Carlos

Carlos is a Filipino violinist and Computer Science student at the University of the Philippines Los Baños (UPLB). He is a resident member of Harmonya and performs with the LWBC String Orchestra, actively participating in church services and ensemble productions. Alongside music, he builds technical projects in software and research. He has developed a mitigation-focused project, created a video game, and presented research at De La Salle University. A former thesis lead at the University of the East, he works with Python, Java, and R. He also serves on the finance team of his church, applying analytical discipline both on stage and in systems.
